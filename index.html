I'm Brad Stricherz

I’m a Geospatial Software Developer based in St. Louis, Missouri, with a passion for leveraging open-source software and publicly available data to solve geospatial challenges. My journey in geospatial technology began in 2009, and since then, I’ve focused on building impactful solutions for spatial analysis, data visualization, and mapping.

I work with nonprofits, NGOs, activists, and academics to create web and mobile applications that address real-world problems.
